About Houcheng Textile

Established in 2005, Houcheng Textile is a company dedicated to the research, development, and production of high-quality textile fabrics. We are equipped with advanced manufacturing facilities and a professional technical team committed to providing superior textile fabric solutions to customers worldwide.

Over years of development, we have become a renowned supplier of TC and CVC fabrics in the industry, with products exported to Europe, America, Southeast Asia, and other regions, earning recognition and trust from numerous customers.

We uphold the business philosophy of "Quality First, Customer Foremost," continuously innovating and pursuing excellence to provide better products and services to our customers.
